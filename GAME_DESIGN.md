# Bahnfish - Game Design Document

## Executive Summary

Bahnfish is a unique fishing game that blends the cozy, relaxing mechanics of Cast n Chill with the atmospheric horror and mystery elements of Dredge. Players experience a seamless day/night cycle where peaceful daytime fishing transforms into tense nighttime expeditions filled with supernatural encounters and rare discoveries.

## Core Pillars

1. **Relaxing Yet Engaging** - Simple controls with depth in strategy
2. **Risk vs Reward** - Push your luck at night for rare catches
3. **Progressive Discovery** - Unlock secrets, locations, and mysteries
4. **Atmospheric Tension** - Dynamic mood shifts between calm and eerie
5. **Meaningful Progression** - Every session advances your capabilities

---

## Game Mechanics

### 1. Fishing System

#### Basic Fishing
- **Two-button control**: Reel in / Let out line
- **Split-screen view**: Above water (boat) / Below water (fish activity)
- **Gentle difficulty curve**: No punishing line breaks for casual play
- **Fish behavior AI**: Different species have unique movement patterns, preferred depths, and bait preferences

#### Advanced Fishing Tools
- **Crab Pots**: Passive income traps for shellfish (set and return later)
- **Drift Nets**: Catch multiple fish while sailing between locations
- **Harpoon**: For large, dangerous catches that can't be reeled normally
- **Dredging Crane**: Recover sunken objects, ship wrecks, and mysterious artifacts

#### Mini-Games
- **Standard Reel**: Timing-based tension management (don't snap the line)
- **Harpoon Strike**: Aim and timing for moving targets
- **Dredge Dodge**: Navigate crane through underwater obstacles to retrieve items

### 2. Day/Night Cycle & Sanity System

#### Daytime (Safe)
- Normal fish spawn rates
- Clear visibility
- No sanity drain
- Standard fish prices
- Tutorial-safe environment

#### Dusk/Dawn (Transition)
- Rare transitional species appear
- Light becomes golden/eerie
- First whispers of the unknown
- Sanity drain begins slowly

#### Nighttime (High Risk/Reward)
- **Sanity Meter**: Depletes while at sea after dark
- **Rare Fish**: Exclusive nighttime species worth 3-5x more
- **Aberrant Fish**: Mutated/supernatural variants with special properties
- **Environmental Hazards**:
  - Swirling vortexes with tentacled horrors that damage your boat
  - Ghost ships that steal your catch
  - Corrupted wildlife (blood-eyed crows, phantom eels)
  - **Fish-stealing creatures**: Crows and phantoms that swoop down and steal fish from your inventory mid-journey
  - **Spontaneous obstacles**: Rocks and debris appear in water at low sanity, forcing navigation skill
  - **Chase sequences**: Massive creatures pursue your boat, requiring speed and evasion
  - Floating eyes that curse your equipment
- **Fog System**: Visibility decreases, navigation becomes challenging
- **Audio Distortion**: Music warps and slows, creating unease

#### Sanity Management
- **Restore at dock**: Sleeping fully restores sanity
- **Lantern upgrades**: Better lights slow sanity drain
- **Talismans**: Consumable items that temporarily halt drain
- **Insanity Events**: At 0 sanity, hallucinations make gameplay unpredictable
  - False fish signals
  - Equipment malfunction
  - Distorted navigation
  - Rare chance of permanent curses (until cleansed)

### 3. Inventory & Resource Management

#### Tetris-Style Inventory
- **Grid-based hull storage**: Different fish shapes fit together like puzzles
- **Space optimization**: Reward efficient packing with bonus storage
- **Cooler slots**: Premium storage that keeps fish fresh longer
- **Equipment slots**: Separate grid for rods, nets, tools
- **Cargo expansion**: Upgradeable through ship modifications

#### Resources
- **Money**: From selling fish, primary progression currency
- **Scrap**: From dredging, used for ship repairs and modifications
- **Relics**: Ancient artifacts that unlock dark abilities
- **Bait Types**: Worms, lures, chum, specialized attractants
- **Fuel**: Limits how far you can travel (risk management)

### 4. Progression Systems

#### Short-Term Loop
1. Buy bait and supplies
2. Travel to fishing location
3. Fish until inventory full or time runs out
4. Return to dock before sanity depletes
5. Sell catch and repair damage
6. Upgrade equipment
7. Repeat

#### Angler's Journal (Collection)
- **50+ Fish Species**: Each with unique behaviors and habitats
- **Trophy System**: Catch personal best for each species
- **Aberrant Variants**: Corrupted versions with special effects
- **Legendary Fish**: Rare bosses requiring strategy to catch
- **Completion Rewards**: Unlock special equipment and abilities

#### Location Licenses
- **13 Distinct Regions**: Each with unique biomes and fish
- **Progressive Unlocking**: Purchase licenses to access new areas
- **Environmental Variety**:
  - Calm lake (starter area)
  - Rocky coastline (medium fish)
  - Deep ocean trenches (rare & dangerous)
  - Fog-shrouded swamp (high aberrant spawn)
  - Arctic waters (endurance challenge)
  - Coral reef (diversity hotspot)
  - Underground cavern (pure mystery/horror)
  - Shipwreck graveyard (dredging focus)
  - And more...

#### Ship Upgrades
- **Hull**: Increased storage capacity and damage resistance
- **Engine**: Faster travel, better escapes from night threats
- **Lights**: Reduced sanity drain, improved night visibility
- **Rod Holders**: Equip multiple rods for different situations
- **Net Capacity**: More passive fishing tools
- **Armor Plating**: Protection from creature attacks
- **Fuel Tank**: Extended range for distant locations

#### Dark Abilities (Dredge-Inspired)
Unlocked by collecting relics and completing mysterious tasks:
- **Abyssal Sprint**: Temporary speed boost to escape danger
- **Tidal Gate**: Teleport between discovered altars
- **Siren's Call**: Attract rare fish to your location
- **Temporal Anchor**: Slow down time during fishing mini-games
- **Eldritch Eye**: See hidden fishing spots and secrets
- **Void Storage**: Temporary extra inventory space (risky - items might vanish)

### 5. Idle/AFK System

- **Auto-Fish Mode**: Character continues fishing when you're away
- **Configurable**: Set rod, bait, and location preferences
- **Offline Progress**: Earn money and catches while not playing
- **Balance**: Auto-mode is less efficient than active play
- **Safety**: Auto-mode only works during day (won't risk night dangers)
- **Notifications**: Alert when inventory is full or supplies depleted

### 6. Mystery & Narrative Elements

#### Environmental Storytelling
- **Abandoned structures**: Hints of what happened to previous fishers
- **Cryptic messages**: Found in bottles, carved on rocks
- **NPC dialogues**: Locals share legends and warnings
- **Journal entries**: Discover logs from lost sailors

#### Quests & Objectives
- **Main Mystery**: Uncover the truth about the region's darkness
- **Character Requests**: NPCs ask for specific fish or items
- **Hidden Altars**: Discover and activate ancient sites
- **Explosive Walls**: Blast through to secret areas (requires specific items)
- **Relic Hunts**: Follow clues to find powerful artifacts

#### Dynamic Events
- **Blood Moon**: Rare event where aberrant fish spawn everywhere
- **Fog Banks**: Random appearance, high risk/reward
- **Meteor Showers**: Temporary buff to fishing luck
- **Festivals**: Town events with special challenges and rewards
- **Migrations**: Seasonal fish movements change what's available

## New & Innovative Features

### 1. Weather System
- **Dynamic Weather**: Rain, storms, fog, clear skies
- **Impact on Gameplay**:
  - Rain attracts certain fish but reduces visibility
  - Storms increase danger but boost rare spawns
  - Clear weather is safest but least rewarding
- **Forecast System**: Plan trips based on 3-day weather prediction

### 2. Cooking & Crafting
- **Cooking Station**: Prepare caught fish for buffs
  - Stamina foods reduce sanity drain
  - Luck meals increase rare fish chance
  - Perception foods reveal hidden spots
- **Bait Crafting**: Combine ingredients for custom lures
- **Equipment Repair**: Maintain gear or face breakdowns

### 3. Aquarium & Exhibition
- **Personal Aquarium**: Keep live specimens of each species
- **Visitors**: NPCs pay to view rare fish in your collection
- **Breeding**: Pair fish to create unique variants (long-term project)
- **Passive Income**: Aquarium generates money over time

### 4. Competitive Elements
- **Fishing Tournaments**: Timed events for biggest catch
- **Leaderboards**: Compare with other players (optional online)
- **Seasonal Challenges**: Limited-time objectives with exclusive rewards
- **Achievement System**: Badges for impressive feats

### 5. Crew & Companion System
- **Hire Crew Members**: NPCs with unique abilities
  - Navigator: Reduces fuel consumption
  - Spotter: Increases fish detection range
  - Engineer: Faster equipment repairs
  - Mystic: Slower sanity drain
- **Crew Management**: Balance cost vs benefits
- **Crew Stories**: Each member has a backstory and questline
- **Pet Companion**: Loyal animal companion that stays with you
  - Dog, cat, or sea creature options
  - **Petting interaction**: Direct emotional connection mechanic
  - Follows player around the boat
  - Provides comfort (slower sanity drain when nearby)
  - Pet customization (appearance, accessories)
  - Always free, never dies - pure emotional support

### 6. Mutations & Genetics
- **Fish Mutations**: Rare genetic variants with unique colors/patterns
- **Mutation Breeding**: Combine traits in aquarium
- **Collector Market**: Special vendors pay premium for perfect mutations
- **Genetic Scanner**: Upgraded tool to identify mutation potential

### 7. Photography Mode
- **Camera Equipment**: Capture photos of fish and environments
- **Photo Challenges**: Specific shots requested by collectors
- **Gallery**: Build a visual journal alongside the written one
- **Rare Moments**: Photograph supernatural events for evidence

### 8. Curse & Blessing System
- **Curses**: Negative effects from night encounters
  - Rotting catch (fish spoil faster)
  - Broken compass (navigation issues)
  - Haunted hull (strange noises, visual glitches)
- **Cleansing**: Visit a mystic to remove curses (costs money/items)
- **Blessings**: Positive buffs from helping NPCs or finding shrines
  - Fisher's luck (increased rare spawns)
  - Calm mind (slower sanity drain)
  - Strong line (reduced break chance)

## Visual & Audio Design

### Art Style
- **Clean, Stylized 3D**: Similar to Cast n Chill's approachable aesthetic
- **Color Palette**:
  - Day: Warm, inviting pastels
  - Dusk/Dawn: Golden oranges and deep purples
  - Night: Desaturated blues with eerie highlights
- **Character Design**: Cozy, relatable protagonist with customization options
- **Fish Design**: Realistic variants with fantastical aberrations

### Audio
- **Ambient Soundscape**: Water lapping, seagulls, gentle waves (day)
- **Dynamic Music**: Transitions from calm acoustic to distorted atmospheric (night)
- **Audio Cues**: Distinct sounds for different fish bites and events
- **Voice Acting**: Minimal but impactful NPC dialogue
- **Horror Elements**: Subtle at first, intensifying with sanity loss

### UI/UX
- **Minimalist HUD**: Only essential information visible
- **Journal Access**: Quick menu for fish encyclopedia and quests
- **Inventory Puzzle**: Satisfying visual feedback for optimal packing
- **Sanity Indicator**: Subtle at high sanity, increasingly urgent at low
- **Tutorial**: Gentle introduction without overwhelming new players

## Risk Mitigation

### Design Risks
- **Balancing cozy vs horror**: Ensure neither overwhelms the other
  - Solution: Clear separation via day/night cycle
- **Complexity creep**: Too many systems might overwhelm players
  - Solution: Gradual unlocking, optional advanced features

### Technical Risks
- **Performance with many systems**: Complex interactions could cause issues
  - Solution: Modular architecture, extensive profiling
- **Save corruption**: Player progression is critical
  - Solution: Robust save system with backups and validation

### Market Risks
- **Niche appeal**: Fishing + horror is unusual combination
  - Solution: Market to both cozy game and horror fans separately
- **Competition**: Other fishing games exist
  - Solution: Unique mechanics and atmosphere differentiate us

## Conclusion

Bahnfish combines the meditative satisfaction of fishing with the thrilling tension of cosmic horror, creating a unique experience that appeals to both casual and hardcore players. By blending proven mechanics from successful titles like Cast n Chill and Dredge, while adding innovative features like aquarium breeding, photography, and crew management, we create a game with exceptional depth and replayability.

The seamless transition from peaceful daytime fishing to nerve-wracking nighttime expeditions creates a dynamic risk-reward loop that keeps players engaged, while the extensive progression systems ensure there's always a new goal to pursue.

With careful balancing and a focus on atmospheric storytelling, Bahnfish has the potential to become a standout indie title that spawns a dedicated community and achieves both critical and commercial success.
