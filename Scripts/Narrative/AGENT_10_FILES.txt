ACT 0: PROLOGUE
- New fisher arrives
- Learn basics from Old Fisher Tom
- First hints of mystery

ACT 1: THE ARRIVAL
- Complete tutorial
- Experience first night
- "Something's wrong with these waters..."
- Meet key NPCs

ACT 2: THE DISCOVERY
- Find first message in bottle
- Aberrant fish appear
- Learn legends of "The Deep One"
- Scientist needs samples
- "The fish... they're changing..."

ACT 3: THE INVESTIGATION
- Discover 3 ancient altars
- Unlock Underground Cavern
- Meet Ghost Fisher (Marcus)
- Learn about failed ritual
- "We should never have disturbed it..."

ACT 4: THE TRUTH
- Find all 5 messages
- Piece together full story:
  * Previous fishers found ancient entity
  * Attempted ritual for control/profit
  * Ritual backfired, cursed waters
  * Entity sleeps but stirs at night
- Three paths emerge

ACT 5: THE RESOLUTION
- GOOD ENDING: Seal the Entity
  * Perform ritual correctly
  * Remove night horror
  * Waters heal

- DARK ENDING: Embrace the Darkness
  * Gain ultimate power
  * Become like Marcus
  * Waters remain cursed

- NEUTRAL ENDING: Ignore Mystery
  * Just keep fishing
  * Status quo continues
  * Life goes on
