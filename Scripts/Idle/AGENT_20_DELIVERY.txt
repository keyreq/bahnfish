IDLE MECHANICS
================================================================================

Auto-Fishing:
  Base Rate: 6 fish/hour
  Max Rate: 48 fish/hour (with all upgrades)

  Rarity Distribution (Idle Only):
    Common: 70%
    Uncommon: 20%
    Rare: 8%
    Legendary: 2%
    Aberrant: 0% (active play only!)

Offline Earnings:
  Base Income: $200/hour
  Max Income: $2,000/hour

  Diminishing Returns:
    0-12 hours: 100% efficiency
    12-18 hours: 80% efficiency
    18-24 hours: 60% efficiency
    24+ hours: Capped by system

Passive Income Sources:
  1. Idle Fishing (requires Auto-Fisher)
  2. Aquarium Exhibition ($10-500/day per fish)
  3. Crew Assignments (requires Crew Autonomy)
  4. Breeding Automation (requires upgrade)
  5. Material Generation (5-20/hour)

Comeback Bonuses:
  24+ hours: +$500
  48+ hours: +$1,000 + 1 Rare Bait
  72+ hours: +$2,000 + 1 Rare Bait + 1 Relic

================================================================================
BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

Idle vs. Active Play:

Active Play:
  • 10-30 fish/hour
  • Access to legendary & aberrant fish
  • Active event participation
  • Skill-based fishing
  • Higher earnings: $500-$1,000/hour

Idle Play:
  • 6-48 fish/hour (upgradeable)
  • Common/uncommon/rare/legendary only
  • Passive income
  • 24-hour cap encourages check-ins
  • Moderate earnings: $200-$2,000/hour

Design Goal: Idle provides 30-50% of active earnings
