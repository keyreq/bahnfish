## FAQ for Store Page

### Q: How long is the game?
A: 30+ hours for main story completion. 60+ hours for 100% completion (all fish, all upgrades, all endings).

### Q: Is this game scary?
A: Bahnfish has horror elements (especially at night) but it's psychological horror, not jump scares. You can adjust difficulty and sanity drain rates. The day portions are completely peaceful.

### Q: Can I just play the peaceful fishing part?
A: Yes! You can stay in daytime-only locations or adjust settings to reduce/remove horror elements. The game respects different playstyles.

### Q: Does the game have multiplayer?
A: Version 1.0 is single-player only. Multiplayer may be added in future updates based on community interest.

### Q: What platforms are supported?
A: PC (Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, Linux Ubuntu 20.04+). Console ports may be considered post-launch.

### Q: Is there controller support?
A: Yes! Full controller support for Xbox, PlayStation, and generic controllers. Keyboard + mouse also fully supported.

### Q: How does the idle system work?
A: If you purchase idle upgrades, your boat continues fishing while you're offline (up to 24 hours). You'll receive a summary of earnings when you return.

### Q: Are there microtransactions or DLC?
A: No microtransactions. All content is included in the base game. Free content updates planned. Potential paid DLC (new locations/fish) may be added later.

### Q: Is this game appropriate for children?
A: Bahnfish is rated [ESRB Rating TBD]. It contains horror themes and psychological elements. Parental discretion advised for younger players.

### Q: What languages are supported?
A: English (full audio and text). Additional languages may be added post-launch.
