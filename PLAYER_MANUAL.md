## Welcome to Bahnfish

*"What lurks beneath the calm waters? What secrets hide in the deep?"*

Bahnfish is a unique blend of cozy fishing and psychological horror. By day, enjoy peaceful fishing across 13 beautiful locations. By night, face cosmic horrors that threaten your sanity. Catch fish, upgrade your boat, uncover dark mysteries, and decide whether to embrace the darkness or resist it.

## Getting Started

### Installation
1. Download Bahnfish from [Steam/Itch.io/etc.]
2. Install to your preferred location
3. Launch the game
4. Adjust settings for your hardware

### First Launch
1. **Tutorial**: First-time players get a 10-minute tutorial
2. **Choose Settings**: Adjust difficulty and accessibility options
3. **Name Your Boat**: Give your vessel a name
4. **Start Fishing**: Begin in the Calm Lake (free starter location)

### Saving Your Game
- **Auto-Save**: Every 5 minutes
- **Manual Save**: Press ESC → Save Game
- **Multiple Saves**: Up to 3 save slots
- **Cloud Saves**: Enabled by default (if platform supports)

## Controls

### Keyboard & Mouse (Default)

#### Movement
- **W/A/S/D**: Move boat forward/left/backward/right
- **Mouse**: Rotate camera
- **Shift**: Sprint (boat speed boost)
- **Space**: Brake

#### Fishing
- **Left Click**: Cast fishing rod
- **Hold Left Click**: Power meter (casting)
- **Left Click (when hooked)**: Reel in
- **Release Left Click**: Let out line (reduce tension)
- **Q/E**: Switch fishing tool

#### Interaction
- **E**: Interact with objects/NPCs
- **E (near companion)**: Pet companion
- **F**: Quick-use item
- **R**: Rotate item in inventory

#### Menus
- **Tab**: Open inventory
- **I**: Open player stats
- **M**: Open map
- **J**: Open journal/quests
- **ESC**: Pause menu

#### Other
- **P**: Photo mode
- **T**: Toggle time of day display
- **H**: Toggle HUD
- **C**: Open crafting menu
- **B**: Open aquarium

### Controller (Xbox/PlayStation)

- **Left Stick**: Move boat
- **Right Stick**: Rotate camera
- **A/X**: Interact/Confirm
- **B/Circle**: Cancel/Back
- **X/Square**: Cast fishing rod
- **Y/Triangle**: Switch tool
- **LB/L1**: Previous menu tab
- **RB/R1**: Next menu tab
- **LT/L2**: Let out line
- **RT/R2**: Reel in
- **D-Pad Up**: Pet companion
- **D-Pad Down**: Quick-use item
- **Start**: Pause menu
- **Select/Share**: Photo mode

### Remapping
All controls can be remapped in Settings → Controls.

## Game Systems

### Economy (3 Currencies)

1. **Money ($)**: Primary currency
   - Earned by selling fish
   - Used for upgrades, licenses, items
   - Night fishing gives 3-5× value (risk/reward)

2. **Scrap (⚙)**: Crafting material
   - Earned from fishing debris and dismantling items
   - Used for crafting and repairs
   - Found in abandoned locations

3. **Relics (✦)**: Rare currency
   - Found in secret areas and deep waters
   - Used for dark abilities and fast travel
   - Unlock supernatural powers

### Time System
- **Day Length**: 15 real minutes = 24 in-game hours (default)
- **Time Periods**: Day (6-18h), Dusk (18-20h), Night (20-6h), Dawn (6-8h)
- **Time Affects**: Fish spawns, NPC availability, sanity drain, prices

### Weather System
- **4 Weather Types**: Clear, Rain, Storm, Fog
- **Weather Effects**: Fish spawn rates, visibility, hazard frequency
- **Forecast**: 3-day weather forecast available

## Fish Species

### 60 Species Across 5 Rarities

#### Common (20 species)
- Value: $5-20
- Spawn Rate: 60%
- Examples: Bass, Trout, Perch

#### Uncommon (15 species)
- Value: $40-70
- Spawn Rate: 25%
- Examples: Salmon, Pike, Catfish

#### Rare (10 species)
- Value: $145-220
- Spawn Rate: 10%
- Examples: Sturgeon, Marlin, Tuna

#### Legendary (5 species)
- Value: $450-520
- Spawn Rate: 4%
- Examples: Giant Squid, Megalodon, Leviathan

#### Aberrant (10 species)
- Value: $80-200
- Spawn Rate: 1%
- Mutations with unique traits
- Only spawn at night or during events

### Fishing Mechanics

1. **Casting**: Hold to power up, release to cast
2. **Waiting**: Wait for a bite (bobber dips)
3. **Hooking**: Click when bobber dips
4. **Reeling**: Active combat!
   - Hold to reel in
   - Release to let out line (reduce tension)
   - Keep tension below 95% or line breaks
   - Fish resist every 2-5 seconds
5. **Caught**: Fish added to inventory

### Fishing Tools (4 types)
- **Fishing Rod**: Standard, versatile
- **Net**: Multiple fish, lower quality
- **Harpoon**: Large/aggressive fish
- **Dredge**: Bottom feeders, relics

## Upgrades & Progression

### Boat Upgrades (9 types)

1. **Fishing Rod** (5 levels, $500-5k)
   - +20% line strength per level

2. **Hull** (4 levels, $1k-8k)
   - +2 grid size per level (10×10 → 15×15)

3. **Engine** (5 levels, $800-6k)
   - +10% speed per level

4. **Lights** (3 levels, $2k-10k)
   - -25% sanity drain per level

5. **Radar** (4 levels, $1.5k-12k)
   - +1 rarity tier visibility

6. **Cargo Capacity** (4 levels, $500-4k)
   - +3 inventory slots per level

7. **Rod Holders** (3 levels, $1k-5k)
   - +1 simultaneous rod per level

8. **Fuel Tank** (3 levels, $800-5k)
   - +50% fuel capacity

9. **Sonar** (4 levels, $2k-15k)
   - Shows fish depth and species

**Total Investment**: ~$100,000 for full upgrades

### Dark Abilities (6 abilities)
Supernatural powers from cursed relics:
- **Night Vision**: See in darkness
- **Fish Sense**: Detect rare fish
- **Void Step**: Short-range teleport
- **Sanity Drain**: Sacrifice sanity for bonuses
- **Soul Harvest**: Bonus from aberrant fish
- **Time Dilation**: Slow time during fishing

**Cost**: 1-5 relics per ability

## Day/Night Cycle

### Day (6:00 - 18:00)
- **Safe**: No sanity drain
- **Normal Fishing**: Standard fish spawns
- **NPCs Available**: Most NPCs open for business
- **Best For**: Exploring, questing, relaxing

### Dusk (18:00 - 20:00)
- **Transition**: Minor sanity drain (0.2/s)
- **Increased Spawns**: More fish active
- **NPCs Closing**: Some NPCs leave

### Night (20:00 - 6:00)
- **Dangerous**: Sanity drain (0.5/s)
- **High Rewards**: 3-5× fish values
- **Night Hazards**: 5 hazard types spawn
- **Aberrant Fish**: Mutated fish appear
- **Best For**: High-risk/high-reward fishing

### Dawn (6:00 - 8:00)
- **Recovery**: Sanity regenerates slowly
- **Peaceful**: Beautiful lighting
- **Bonus Spawns**: Some rare fish more common

## Sanity System

### Sanity Meter (0-100)
- **High Sanity (70-100)**: Normal gameplay
- **Medium Sanity (30-70)**: Minor visual effects
- **Low Sanity (<30)**: Intense effects, hazards spawn

### Sanity Drain
- **Day**: No drain
- **Dusk**: 0.2/s
- **Night**: 0.5/s (reduced with light upgrades)
- **Cursed Fish**: -5 to -15 sanity
- **Horror Events**: Additional drain

### Sanity Regeneration
- **Sleep**: Full restore
- **Shore Proximity**: +0.2/s
- **Cooking**: +5 to +20 (meals)
- **Companion Petting**: +5 (if sanity < 50%)
- **Safe Locations**: Slow regeneration

### Low Sanity Effects
- Screen shake and distortion
- Chromatic aberration
- Color desaturation
- Audio hallucinations
- UI glitches
- Fish ghosting

### Night Hazards (5 types)
1. **Fish Thief**: Steals fish from inventory
2. **Obstacles**: Floating debris damages boat
3. **Fog**: Reduced visibility
4. **Ghost Ship**: Chase sequence
5. **Whisperer**: Audio disturbances

## Cooking & Crafting

### Cooking System

**30+ Recipes** across 5 tiers:
- Tier 1: Basic meals ($5-15, 10-15 min buffs)
- Tier 2: Improved meals ($30-60, 15-20 min buffs)
- Tier 3: Advanced meals ($100-180, 20-25 min buffs)
- Tier 4: Rare delicacies ($300-450, 25-30 min buffs)
- Tier 5: Mythic feasts ($600+, 40+ min buffs)

### 8 Buff Types
1. **Fishing Luck**: +10-50% rare fish chance
2. **Line Strength**: +15-40% tension capacity
3. **Speed Boost**: +20-60% boat speed
4. **Sanity Shield**: -50-100% sanity drain
5. **Night Vision**: See in darkness
6. **Coin Multiplier**: +10-50% fish value
7. **XP Boost**: +25-75% progression speed
8. **Weather Resistance**: Reduce weather effects

**Buff Stacking**: Same type refreshes duration, different types stack (max 10).

### Crafting System

**20+ Recipes**:
- **Bait** (8 types): Worm, Minnow, Grub, Leech, Squid, Crab, Shrimp, Lure
- **Tools** (6 types): Net, Harpoon, Dredge, Trap, Spear, Cage
- **Upgrades** (6 types): Reinforced Line, Sharper Hook, Better Reel, etc.

### Preservation (4 methods)
- **None**: 48 hours before spoilage
- **Ice Box**: 7 days ($500 upgrade)
- **Salting**: 14 days ($1,000 + salt)
- **Smoking**: 30 days ($2,000 + fuel)
- **Freezing**: Indefinite ($5,000 upgrade)

## Aquarium & Breeding

### Aquarium System

**8 Tank Types**:
1. **Small** ($500, 5 fish): Starter, Desktop
2. **Medium** ($2,000, 10 fish): Community, Themed
3. **Large** ($5,000, 20 fish): Exhibition, Breeding
4. **Massive** ($15,000, 50 fish): Museum, Aberrant

**6 Upgrade Categories**:
- Capacity (+5 fish per level)
- Auto-Feeder (reduce maintenance)
- Filtration (+7.5% quality per level)
- Lighting (unlock rare displays)
- Breeding Chamber (faster cycles)
- Genetics Lab (+0.5% mutation per level)

### Breeding System

**Genetics**: 10 inheritable traits
1. Size (0.5×-1.5×)
2. Color (8 variants: Natural, Golden, Albino, Melanistic, Blue, Red, Purple, Rainbow)
3. Pattern (Solid, Spots, Stripes)
4. Rarity Bonus (0-15%)
5. Value Modifier (0.7×-1.6×)
6. Aggression (0-1)
7. Growth Rate (0.5×-2×)
8. Lifespan (20-180 days)
9. Bioluminescence (recessive)
10. Mutation Chance (1-5%)

**Breeding Process**:
- Select 2 fish in breeding tank
- Pay breeding cost ($50-200)
- Wait 24 real hours
- 30-80% success rate
- Offspring inherits traits

**Collection Goal**: 480+ variants (60 species × 8 colors × 3 patterns)

### Passive Income
- Exhibition fees: $100-2,000/day
- Depends on fish rarity and tank quality

## Idle Progression

### How It Works
When you're offline, your boat continues fishing automatically (if upgrades purchased).

- **Rarity**: Common 70%, Uncommon 20%, Rare 8%, Legendary 2%
- **No Aberrant Fish**: Aberrant only spawn during active play

### Idle Upgrades (10 tiers, $265k total)
1. **Auto-Fisher** ($5k): Enable idle fishing
2. **Auto-Sell** ($2k): Auto-sell when inventory full
3. **Quality Rod Holders Lvl 1-3** ($5k-15k each): +20% rate each
4. **Efficiency Boost Lvl 1-3** ($10k-30k each): +10% income each
5. **Time Compression Lvl 1-2** ($20k-50k each): 1.5×, 2× speed
6. **Storage Expansion** ($10k-100k): Increase cap
7. **Crew Autonomy** ($10k): Crew work while offline

### Offline Earnings

- **Full Build** ($265k): $2,000/hour, 5.5 days ROI

  - 24h: +$500

### Passive Income Sources

## Tips & Strategies

### For Beginners
1. **Take the Tutorial**: Learn the basics
2. **Fish During Day First**: Get comfortable before night
3. **Upgrade Lights Early**: Reduces night sanity drain
4. **Save Often**: Don't lose progress
5. **Pet Your Dog**: Free sanity boost!

### For Economy
1. **Night Fishing = Big Money**: 3-5× values but risky
2. **Bulk Sell Bonus**: Sell 10+ fish for +5-15% bonus
3. **Cook Before Selling**: Some recipes worth more
4. **Breeding Profit**: Breed rare fish, sell offspring
5. **Idle Upgrades**: Best ROI for passive income

### For Combat (Fishing)
1. **Watch Tension Meter**: Keep below 95%
2. **Let Out Line**: Release during fish resistance
3. **Use Right Tool**: Harpoon for aggressive fish
4. **Upgrade Rod Early**: +20% line strength per level
5. **Use Buffs**: Line Strength buff makes catching easier

### For Sanity Management
1. **Avoid Night**: Or upgrade lights first
2. **Stay Near Shore**: Slow regeneration
3. **Cook Meals**: Sanity Shield buff helpful
4. **Pet Companion**: +5 sanity if low
5. **Return to Dock**: Sleep for full restore

### For Collection
1. **Photography First**: Easier to catalog
2. **Breeding Lab**: Genetics upgrades help
3. **Bait Preferences**: Use preferred bait for species
4. **Event Fishing**: Rare fish during events
5. **Aberrant Hunting**: Blood Moon for aberrants

### For Late Game
1. **Max Upgrades**: ~$100k total investment
2. **Dark Abilities**: 6 abilities cost relics
3. **Endgame Locations**: Abyssal Trench ($10k)
4. **Full Collection**: 480+ fish variants
5. **All Challenges**: $50k+ from photo challenges

## Troubleshooting

### Performance Issues
- **Low FPS**: Reduce quality settings
- **Stuttering**: Enable VSync or cap frame rate
- **Memory Issues**: Reduce texture quality
- **Long Load Times**: Install on SSD

### Audio Issues
- **No Sound**: Check device selection in settings
- **Audio Crackling**: Increase buffer size
- **Missing Sounds**: Verify game files

### Save Issues
- **Can't Save**: Check disk space (need 100MB free)
- **Corrupted Save**: Game has 3 automatic backups
- **Missing Progress**: Check correct save slot

### Controls Issues
- **Controller Not Detected**: Enable controller in settings
- **Keys Not Working**: Check key bindings
- **Input Lag**: Reduce input buffer in settings

### Game Won't Launch
- **Check System Requirements**: See minimum specs
- **Update Graphics Drivers**: Latest drivers recommended
- **Verify Game Files**: Use platform's verify feature
- **Run as Administrator**: Try if permissions issues
